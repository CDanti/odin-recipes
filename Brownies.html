Brownies

In this article I'm going to teach you how to make Chris' famous brownies. All the stoners and alcoholics love them!


Ingredients

  • Box Bronie mix
  • Half a cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of water

Note: To spice things up, you can add chocolate chips or a tsp of coffee grounds. I love adding coffee grounds to my brownies.

Instructions

  1. Remove the brownie mix from the box and preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Get your mixing bowl and pour the brownie mix into it.
  3. Now mix in the eggs, water, and olive oil into the mixing bowl
  4. Mix ingredients until unified
  5. Grease the pan for the brownies and pour in the brownie mixture
  6. Put the greased pan with the brownie mix in the oven for around 40 minutes. Check on the brownies and do the Fork/Toothpick Test until completed
