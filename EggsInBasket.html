Egg's In A Basket

In this article I'm going to teach you how to make Chris' signature breakfast of Egg's In A Basket using an Air Fryer.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Egg
  • Shreded cheese (any kind will do, I reccomend Mozzarella)

Steps:

  1. Use a cup to make a circular indent in the center of the bread.
  2. Crack open the egg into the circular indent in the bread.
  3. Sprinkle shreded cheese around the cracked egg/circular indent and on the bread.
  4. Place into the Air Fryer for 8 minutes on 370 degrees.
  5. Enjoy!
